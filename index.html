Welcome to Vinyaas Public School

The School is founded with an aim to be a Centre of excellence among the education provider in the city of Kanpur. Vinyaas Public School is located in outskirts of Kanpur at Amiliha, Chaubepur Kanpur Nagar on the main G.T road, 8kms away from I.I.T Kanpur. School sprants in lush green 20 acres of land with a playground for athletics and other games/ sports. The school building has spacious and well ventilated class rooms fixed with student friendly furniture to provide excellent environment for vibrant learning.
